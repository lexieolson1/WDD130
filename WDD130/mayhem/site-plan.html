Overview

Purpose

My purpose for this website is to create an easy way for customers to find and purchase make up online. This is specifically makeup inspired by rock legends of the ages, and will be engineered to be highly pigmented and professional.

Audience

My audience will be people who would like to learn more and/or purchase a specific line of make up that can be worn onstage and honors rock legends of the ages.

Content

home page

Makeup is art. It can make a statement, invoke emotion, and even create new characters. In many different professions, it is used to represent what you are about. Drag queens, cosplayers, rock and roll icons, and makeup artists on social media to name a few all use makeup to promote their work and create art. Inspired by alternative rock icons throughout the ages, we created Mayhem. A brand of makeup for those who want to express themselves boldly. We wanted to create a brand that is professional but still affordable so everyone can experiment with their look. Our makeup is high quality and gives anyone the opportunity to create. It is designed to be pigmented. This is how it creates bold looks so effortlessly. We are vegan and cruelty-free, if you have any questions or concerns, please contact our customer support team below.
